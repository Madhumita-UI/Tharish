Our Story

About Tharish Dental Clinic

Founded in 2009 by two visionary specialists, Tharish Dental Clinic was built on a single promise — to deliver world-class oral care with genuine compassion. Today, our founders continue to lead every aspect of clinical excellence.

Our Mission

To make exceptional dental care accessible, pain-free, and transparent — treating every patient as family, not a file number.

Our Vision

To be Southeast Asia's most trusted multi-specialty dental centre, setting the gold standard for clinical outcomes and patient experience.

Our Values

Integrity in every diagnosis, precision in every procedure, and empathy in every conversation — before, during, and after treatment.
